Intern at X29.ai

Play a Role in Engineering the AI Native Enterprise

For Up and Coming AI Researchers & Builders

At X29.ai, we believe the enterprise of the future won’t just “adopt AI” — it will be redesigned around it. Just as the Grumman X-29 tested the edges of flight with experimental aerodynamics, our lab tests the edges of what enterprises can become in an AI-first world.

That takes research. That takes building. And we’re calling for both.

We’re opening 10 internship spots:

  • 5x AI Research Interns
  • 5x AI Agent Interns

Track One: AI Research Interns

Help us explore what it truly means for a company to be AI-native. Each research intern will choose a subtrack — Sales, Marketing, Operations, Customer Support, or HR — and focus their efforts there.

You’ll work with our team to:

  • Investigate how AI could reshape your chosen function.
  • Identify workflows best suited for automation, and explain why AI should do it.
  • Spot the tasks where humans must stay in the loop — and why replacing them would weaken outcomes.
  • Draft research briefs that map the balance of AI and human effort in your area.
  • Contribute to company-level AI-native designs that show how entire enterprises should be structured around AI.

Who should apply: If you’re a strong writer, a sharp researcher, and curious about how work gets divided between people and machines, this is your track.

‍

Track Two: AI Builder Interns

Turn research into reality. While the research interns identify which workflows should be automated, you’ll be the ones actually trying to automate them.

You’ll get hands-on with agent tools and workflows to:

  • Prototype AI agents directly on the workflows flagged by the research track as “automation-worthy.”
  • Build and test systems across Sales, Marketing, Operations, Customer Support, and HR.
  • Experiment with multi-agent setups, copilots, and automations — stress-testing where they work and where they fail.
  • Run small pilots to see how agents perform in practice, not just in theory.
  • Help create new playbooks for how agents can transform work at scale.

Who should apply: If you like to tinker, build, and break things to see what’s possible — this is your track.

‍

What You’ll Get

  • Front-row seat at the frontier — Join live projects with CIOs, founders, and transformation leads.
  • Hands-on impact — Whether you’re writing research or building agents, your work will shape deliverables used by enterprises.
  • Mentorship — Learn how to connect AI research, design, and implementation in a way few people are doing today.
  • Future pathways — Internships often lead to paid roles or collaborations; at minimum, you’ll walk away with rare skills and strong portfolio pieces.

‍

The Details

  • Location: Remote-first (Sydney HQ, global partners).
  • Time: Flexible part-time, enough to contribute meaningfully each week.
  • Duration: 3–4 months, with extension opportunities.
  • Compensation: Unpaid — this is about access, learning, and contribution. (Paid roles often emerge for standout interns.)

‍

How to Apply

Send us a short note on:

  1. Which track you’re applying for (Research or Builder).
  2. Why you want to explore AI-native enterprise design.
  3. Something you’ve written, built, or researched that shows your curiosity.

Email us at contact@x29.ai with subject line: Internship Application – Research [or Builder] – [Your Name]

‍

Final Word

Like the X-29, our lab exists to test the impossible and turn instability into new maneuverability. If you want to be one of the first 10 to help engineer the AI-native enterprise, now’s your chance.

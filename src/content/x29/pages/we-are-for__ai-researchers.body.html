For AI Leads, AI Researchers and the Next Generation

Why AI Leaders Need a Shared Lab

AI transformation leads face an immense challenge: the technology evolves faster than standards can form. Each week brings new tools, new models, and new risks. Without collaboration, leaders repeat the same mistakes, learning in isolation and losing valuable time.

At X29.ai, we exist in part to change that. Our lab is where AI leaders, researchers, and agencies connect, share learnings, and refine approaches together. Instead of re-running the same failed experiments, members gain insight from peers, avoid costly detours, and accelerate adoption.

We are now seeking 10x AI Transformation Leads / CIOs to join the X29.ai lab — pioneers ready to set new standards for the AI-native enterprise.

Opportunities for Interns

We open the lab to the next generation of talent. Interns don’t just study AI — they help apply it to core business workflows. It’s hands-on, applied learning that creates new standards for how AI enters the enterprise.

We are seeking 5x AI Research Interns to join research projects across Sales, Marketing, Operations, HR, and Customer Support.

Alongside them, 5x AI Agent Interns — building real prototype agents that test real-world use cases and delivery models. Working side-by-side with research interns, these interns push the frontier of what’s possible, creating a new kind of collaborative learning environment.

AI Agencies + X29.ai

Scaling an AI agency without standardised offerings is almost impossible. Each client feels like a new custom build, delivery is hard to manage across accounts, and communicating value to stakeholders is an uphill battle.

That’s why the lab is also open to 5x AI Agencies. Through Conducting.ai, we productise our research into building plans and frameworks agencies can repeatedly deploy. This gives them clarity for clients, delivery discipline for teams, and visual narratives that communicate ROI to every stakeholder.

The Outcome

For transformation leads, interns, and agencies alike, X29.ai is a shared testbed. It’s where knowledge compounds, standards form, and the next generation of enterprises are built.

Closing Thought

The Grumman X-29 was built to prove that instability could become advantage. At X29.ai, we extend that philosophy to enterprise AI — creating a lab where leaders connect, interns learn, and agencies scale, all building together toward the operating models of the future. The testbed for the future of enterprise. Welcome to X29.ai.

Heritage: Inspired by the Grumman X-29

Grumman x-29 Test Flight
In the 1980s, the Grumman X-29 was one of the most radical aircraft ever built. With its forward-swept wings, canard controls, and unstable aerodynamics, it shouldn’t have been able to fly. Yet with the right mix of composite materials and fly-by-wire systems, the X-29 proved that what looked impossible on paper could be engineered into reality.

At X29.ai, we take that same spirit of experimentation into the world of business. Like the jet that inspires our name, we embrace instability, test unconventional designs, and prove the future today. We don’t just adapt AI to existing systems — we redesign enterprises around it.
